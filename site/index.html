OCRe
The ultimate OCR by HUGE Software
OCRe is an Optical Character Recognition software application. It is developed by a group of 4 Epita students. Epita is the Graduate School of Computer Science and Advanced Technologies located in Paris, France.

OCRe is developed in C and OCaml, two fantastic programming languages. This project is due for the end of May 2008.
